Dive into skippity rock, a relaxing mobile game where you do one thing: skip a rock. With one tap, send your rock skipping across the sea and try to land on the next platform to sail to new shores.

Collect chests along the way to unlock new characters, such as pirates, mermaids, scuba divers and more, each with its own lighthearted personality.

The game has a gentle rhythm, making it easy to pick up and play whenever you need a break. The ocean is full of little surprises, sure to make your day a little calmer and brighter.


Key Features:

  • Casual, one-tap gameplay.
  • Peaceful environments that change each game.
  • Unlockable sea-themed characters that add charm to your adventure.
  • Soothing soundtrack featuring gentle waves and ocean breezes.
  • A laid-back pace with no time limits or pressure—just skip and relax.

skippity rock is an enjoyable experience that will let you relax and laugh as you skip your way through the ocean.


Target Platforms: Android and iOS

Scheduled Release: Q2 of 2025
