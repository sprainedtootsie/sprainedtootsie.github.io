Dive into skippity rock, a relaxing, casual mobile game where you do one thing: skip a rock. To play: tap, hold, and release. The longer you hold for, the farther your rock skips. Skip your rock just far enough to land on the next island. Sail to the island, and go again!

The further you sail, the more random the size and space between islands becomes, providing just enough of a challenge to stay engaged. Slowly build your skill as you earn points and collect treasure to unlock new characters such as pirates and mermaids.


Key Features:

  • Casual, one-tap gameplay.
  • Peaceful environments.
  • Unlockable sea-themed characters.
  • Soothing soundtrack with ocean waves and breezes.
  • A laid-back pace with no time limits or pressure.

skippity rock is an enjoyable experience that will let you relax and laugh as you skip your way through the ocean.


Target Platforms: Android and iOS
