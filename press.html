Dive into skippity rock, a relaxing mobile game where you do one thing: skip a rock. With one tap, send your rock skipping across the sea and try to land on the next platform.

Sail to new shores as you collect chests along the way to unlock new characters such as pirates and mermaids.

The game has a gentle rhythm, making it easy to pick up and play whenever you need a break. The ocean is full of little surprises, sure to make your day a little calmer and brighter.


Key Features:

  • Casual, one-tap gameplay.
  • Peaceful environments.
  • Charming sea-themed characters.
  • Soothing soundtrack featuring gentle waves and ocean breezes.
  • A laid-back pace with no time limits or pressure—just skip and relax.

skippity rock is an enjoyable experience that will let you relax and laugh as you skip your way through the ocean.


Target Platforms: Android and iOS

Scheduled Release: Q2 of 2025 on Android

Tags: casual, mobile gaming, relaxing, endless, sea-themed, simple mechanics, single-player, arcade
